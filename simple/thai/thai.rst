Thai language
=============

Everyday phrases
----------------

Greetings:
    - Hello/Goog morning/Good evening: **sa-wat-di:**
    - How are You?: **sa-bai-di**
    - I am fine: **sa-bai**

In a shop:
    - How much is this?: **ni-thao-rai**

Say thanks:
    - Never mind, It's not big deal: **mai-bpen-rai**

To be polite, man add **khrap** (**khap**) at the end of statement.

Numbers
-------

0 - 9:
    - 0: **su:n**
    - 1: **neung**
    - 2: **sa:wng**
    - 3: **sa:m**
    - 4: **se:**
    - 5: **ha:**
    - 6: **hohk**
    - 7: **jet**
    - 8: **bpa:et**
    - 9: **ga:o**

10 - 1000 000:
    - 10: **sip**
    - 100: **r^a:wy**
    - 1000: **phon**
    - 10 000: **me_u:n**
    - 100 000: **sa:en**
    - 1000 000: **la:n**
