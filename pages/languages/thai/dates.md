# Dates

## Days of a week

Sunday - wan Athit
Monday - wan Jan
Tuesday - wan Ankhaan
Wednesday - wan Phut
Thursday - wan Phar(l)euhat
Friday - wan Suk
Saturday - wan Sau

## Relative

Phroo nii - tomorrow
one nii - today
m–∏ one nii - yesterday
con ... - before
chrn - half
one yut - day off

## Time

To nee kii moony khrap? - what time is it? (to nee - now, kii moony - what time)
Na tee - minute
Eak 30 nat tee - 30 minutes more
