## Greetings

Sabaai dii mai khap? - How are you?
Laew kun la khap? - And you?

## Basic conversation

Khun ma chak nai khap? - Where are you from?
Khun chee arrai khap? - What is your name?
Mai khap - no.
Pa sa Thai riot arrai khap.
