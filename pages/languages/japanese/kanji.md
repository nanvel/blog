# Kanji

Kanji are Chinese characters used in Japanese. About 2500 of them are in common use.

土 (つち) - soil
天 (テン) - sky
夕 (ゆう) - evening
口 (くち) - mouth
何 (なに) - what
皆 (みんな) - everyone (in this room, for instance)
私 (わたし) - I am
国 (くに) - Country


## Male or female

Male: 男子, 紳士
Female: 女子, 婦人
